My Best Friends

A tribute to my Friends

I want to honor the incredible people who hold this special place in my heart, my best friends, who are more than just companions; they are family by choice. Each of them is so different, yet they fit perfectly into my life. There’s the one who can make me laugh until my stomach hurts, turning even the worst day into something I can smile about. Then there’s the one who always knows the right thing to say, the one who listens and somehow makes all my problems seem smaller. And of course, there’s the steady, reliable one, the friend who feels like a rock when everything else feels like it’s falling apart. I remember one time—just a random day—we decided go to one of our friends house. No plan, it's just so sudden then we bought alcoholic drinks. That’s the thing about them: even the simplest moments become unforgettable. We didn’t need anything fancy; we had each other, and that was enough. It’s not just the fun times, though. They’ve seen me at my absolute worst—the days when I felt like giving up, when I couldn’t see the light at the end of the tunnel. And yet, they never turned away. They stayed. They reminded me who I was when I couldn’t remember. They’ve been there for all the big moments, too. Celebrating my wins like they were their own, pushing me to dream bigger, and always believing in me, even when I doubted myself. I honestly don’t know where I’d be without them. But what I love most about my friends is that they love me for me. I don’t have to pretend or put on a front. With them, I can be silly, messy, emotional, or completely myself, and they wouldn’t want me any other way. We’ve built a lifetime of memories together, and I know there are so many more to come. Late-night talks that turn into early mornings, spontaneous adventures, and even those quiet moments where we don’t need to say a word—we’ve shared it all. So, this is my thank you to them. Thank you for being my people, for standing by me through everything, for making me laugh when I needed it most, and for loving me just as I am. I hope they know how much they mean to me because, truly, they’re the best thing life has given me. If there’s one thing I’ve learned, it’s that friendships like ours don’t come around often. I’m lucky and blessed to have them and I’ll never take that for granted. Here’s to my best friends, the ones who make life worth it. I love you all more than words can say.
